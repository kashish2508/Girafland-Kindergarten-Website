MISSION & VISSION

[ Girafland kindergarten ]

VISSION

We want to see a society in which learning and education mean thinking deeply about purpose of life, sharing freedom, and spreading hope in the community, nation and the world. We hope the generations emerging out of our schools will make shape such a society.

MISSION

Our mission is to cultivate a community of families and teachers who work together to provide a safe, stimulating and positive growth and learning environment to children.
